# Batting Cage - Home Run Teddy

## Overview
A timing-based baseball game. Watch the pitch, time your swing, and knock it out of the park!

## How to Play
1. Watch the ball coming toward you
2. TAP/SPACE when the ball is in the strike zone
3. Better timing = better hits!

## Hit Results
- **Home Run**: Perfect timing = 50 points
- **Double**: Great timing = 25 points
- **Single**: Good timing = 15 points
- **Foul**: Okay timing = 5 points
- **Whiff**: Miss = 0 points
- **Strike**: Don't swing = 0 points

## Pitch Types
- **Slow**: Easy to time
- **Fast**: Quick pitch
- **Curve**: Ball curves mid-flight
- **Lightning**: Super fast
- **Change-up**: Variable speed
- **Slither**: Wavy movement

## Tips
1. Watch the ball, not the batter
2. Swing when the ball enters the dotted zone
3. Rhythm mode: Swing on the beat for 1.5x points!

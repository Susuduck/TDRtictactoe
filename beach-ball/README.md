# Beach Ball - Keep It Up!

A ball-bouncing arcade game where you tap to keep the beach ball in the air.

## How to Play

1. Click or tap the ball to bop it upward
2. Keep the ball from hitting the ground
3. Each successful bop adds to your score
4. Reach the target score to complete the level

## Game Mechanics

- **Gravity**: The ball constantly falls downward
- **Speed Increase**: The ball speeds up over time
- **Shrinking Hit Zone**: The tap target area shrinks as you play
- **Perfect Hits**: Tap close to the center for bonus points

## Opponents

Each of the 10 opponents introduces unique ball physics:

1. **Funky Frog** - Basic bouncing mechanics
2. **Cheeky Chicken** - Perfect hits give bonus points
3. **Disco Dinosaur** - Ball pulses to the beat
4. **Radical Raccoon** - Wind blows the ball sideways
5. **Electric Eel** - Ball splits into two
6. **Mysterious Moth** - Ball fades in and out
7. **Professor Penguin** - Ice zones slow the ball
8. **Sly Snake** - Ball curves unpredictably
9. **Wolf Warrior** - Gravity direction shifts
10. **Grand Master Grizzly** - All challenges combined

## Progression

- 10 levels per opponent
- 4 points = 1 star
- 40 points (10 stars) to master an opponent
- Must master previous opponent to unlock the next

## Controls

- **Click/Tap**: Bop the ball upward
- **Escape**: Return to menu

## Technical Details

- Built with React 18
- Progress saved to localStorage (`beachball_progression_v1`)

# Honey Catch (Honey Pot Drop)

A catching game where you move left/right to catch falling items and earn points.

## How to Play

1. Use **Arrow Keys** or **A/D** to move left and right
2. On mobile, **touch and drag** to move
3. Catch **honey pots** for points
4. Avoid **bees** and **rocks** for penalties
5. Build combos by catching consecutive good items
6. Reach the target score within 60 seconds to win

## Items

| Item | Points | Description |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Honey Pot | +10 | Standard good item |
| Golden Honey | +30 | Rare bonus item |
| Bee | -15 | Avoid! |
| Rock | -25 | Big penalty! |

## Opponents

Each opponent introduces unique mechanics:

1. **Funky Frog** - Basic slow falling, just honey pots
2. **Cheeky Chicken** - Introduces bees to avoid
3. **Disco Dinosaur** - Items speed up over time
4. **Radical Raccoon** - Introduces rocks, items zig-zag
5. **Electric Eel** - Golden honey appears, items move in waves
6. **Mysterious Moth** - Some items hidden until close
7. **Professor Penguin** - Items fall in groups and patterns
8. **Sly Snake** - Items can change direction mid-fall
9. **Wolf Warrior** - Multiple items at once, faster pace
10. **Grand Master Grizzly** - All mechanics combined

## Progression

- Earn 1-4 points per victory based on performance
- 4 points = 1 star
- 10 stars (40 points) = Master an opponent
- Must master previous opponent to unlock next
- Progress saved to localStorage

## Controls

- **Arrow Keys / A,D**: Move left/right
- **ESC**: Pause game
- **Touch/Drag**: Mobile movement

## Theme

Pink and gold color scheme with accent color #ffd700.

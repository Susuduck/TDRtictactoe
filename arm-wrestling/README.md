# Arm Wrestling

## Overview
A rapid-tap battle game where you compete against 10 unique opponents in arm wrestling matches. Tap as fast as you can to overpower your opponent, but watch your stamina!

## How to Play
1. **Select an Opponent**: Choose from 10 unlockable opponents
2. **Choose a Level**: Each opponent has 10 levels of increasing difficulty
3. **TAP!**: Rapidly tap the screen, click, or press SPACE to build power
4. **Win**: Fill your power bar to 100% before your opponent does

## Controls
- **SPACE / ENTER**: Tap to build power
- **Mouse Click**: Tap to build power
- **Touch Screen**: Tap anywhere to build power
- **ESC**: Return to previous screen

## Scoring System
- **Win**: +2 Points
- **Loss**: +0 Points
- **4 Points = 1 Star**
- **40 Points (10 Stars) = Opponent Mastered**

## Stamina System
- Tapping too fast (8+ taps/second) drains stamina
- Low stamina reduces your power gain
- Stamina regenerates slowly over time

## Opponents

| # | Name | Gimmick |
|---|------|---------|
| 1 | Funky Frog | No special moves |
| 2 | Cheeky Chicken | Feathers obscure view briefly |
| 3 | Disco Dinosaur | Gets power surges on the beat |
| 4 | Radical Raccoon | Your stamina drains faster |
| 5 | Electric Eel | Can stun you briefly |
| 6 | Mysterious Moth | Screen darkens periodically |
| 7 | Professor Penguin | Quick math questions pause action |
| 8 | Sly Snake | Can steal some of your progress |
| 9 | Wolf Warrior | Gets aggressive power bursts |
| 10 | Grand Master Grizzly | Uses all gimmicks randomly |

## Difficulty Scaling
- **Opponent 1**: AI taps at ~2 taps/sec
- **Opponent 10**: AI taps at ~10 taps/sec with all gimmicks

Each opponent has 10 levels that progressively increase:
- AI tap speed
- AI power per tap
- Gimmick frequency
- Recovery rate when losing

## Progression
- Beat an opponent's Level 1 to earn stars
- Earn 10 stars on an opponent to unlock the next one
- Earn 40 points total on an opponent to "Master" them

## Tips
- Don't tap too fast or you'll exhaust your stamina
- Watch for gimmicks and react quickly
- Build a steady rhythm rather than mashing randomly
- Professor Penguin's quizzes: answer correctly for bonus power!

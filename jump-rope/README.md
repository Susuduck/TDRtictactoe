# Jump Rope - Skip Teddy

## Overview
A rhythm-based jumping game. Time your jumps to skip over the rope as it swings around. Each opponent introduces unique rhythm challenges.

## Design Philosophy
- **Pattern Learning**: Rhythm patterns become internalized
- **New Mechanics Per Opponent**: Different timing challenges
- **Clear Feedback**: Visual timing indicator, instant results
- **Flow**: Speed increases naturally, keeping you in the zone

## How to Play
1. Watch the rope swing in rhythm
2. TAP when the rope is at the bottom
3. Jump over it!
4. Miss = game over

## Controls
- **SPACE**: Jump
- **Click/Touch**: Jump
- **ESC**: Return to menu

## Rhythm Mechanics

| Opponent | BPM | Mechanic |
|----------|-----|----------|
| Funky Frog | 60 | Steady rhythm - learn the beat |
| Cheeky Chicken | 70 | **Perfect timing** = bonus! |
| Disco Dinosaur | 75 | **Accelerating** - speed up! |
| Radical Raccoon | 80 | **Syncopation** - off-beat |
| Electric Eel | 85 | **Double-tap** for some jumps |
| Mysterious Moth | 90 | **Blind mode** - rely on audio |
| Professor Penguin | 95 | **Patterns** - skip some beats |
| Sly Snake | 100 | **Irregular timing** |
| Wolf Warrior | 90 | **Double dutch** - two ropes! |
| Grand Master Grizzly | 110 | **ALL CHALLENGES!** |

## Scoring
- Each successful jump: +1
- Perfect timing (in green zone): +1 bonus
- Get 5+ perfect jumps for extra star!

## Win Conditions
- Reach target jump count
- Don't miss any jumps!

## Tips
1. Watch the timing indicator at the bottom
2. Jump when it turns GREEN
3. Listen to the beat sound - rhythm is key
4. For double-tap jumps, tap twice quickly
5. In blind mode, close your eyes and feel the rhythm
6. Pattern mode: JUMP-JUMP-JUMP-SKIP rhythm

## Why This Game is Fun
- **Simple Input**: One button, pure timing
- **Rhythm Flow**: Gets into your head
- **Increasing Challenge**: Speed ramps naturally
- **Clear Goals**: Jump count always visible
- **Satisfying Feedback**: Perfect jumps feel great!
